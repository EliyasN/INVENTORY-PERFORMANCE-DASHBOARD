📊 Inventory Performance Dashboard

To analyze and monitor inventory operations through interactive visualizations and key business metrics. 
It provides valuable insights into stock movement, revenue, expenses, profitability, and supplier performance, 
enabling efficient inventory management and informed business decision-making.

📂 Dataset Overview

📅 Date     – Records the transaction date.
🗓️ Month    – Enables monthly performance analysis.
📦 Product  – Identifies the inventory item.
🏷️ Category – Classifies products into categories.
📥 Stock In – Quantity of inventory received.
📤 StockOut – Quantity of inventory issued or sold.
💰 Revenue  – Total sales generated.
💸 Expenses – Total inventory-related costs.
🚚 Supplier – Supplier information.
📈 Profit   – Net profit earned.
📊 Profit Margin – Profit percentage based on revenue.

✨ Key Features

 📊 Interactive Dashboard
📈 KPI Performance Monitoring
📋 Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
🎛️ Dynamic Slicers and Filters
📦 Inventory Tracking & Analysis
💹 Revenue, Expenses & Profit Analysis
⚡ Automated Excel Calculations
📑 Professional Business Reporting

🎯 Summary

To provide a comprehensive inventory analysis solution that helps businesses monitor inventory performance, 
optimize stock levels, evaluate financial metrics, and support data-driven decision-making using Microsoft Excel.
